# Atlas Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Atlas ("Atlas", "the bot", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and deletes information when users interact with Atlas on Discord.

## 1. What Atlas Does

Atlas is a Discord bot that provides moderation, security, support, community, dashboard, and economy features for Discord servers.

## 2. Information We Collect

Atlas may process the following types of information in order to provide its features:

- Discord user IDs
- Discord server IDs
- Discord channel IDs
- Discord role IDs
- Discord message content when required for moderation, automod, support, and command handling
- Moderation records such as warnings, cases, appeals, staff notes, and audit events
- Ticket records such as ticket owners, subjects, responses, assignments, and close reasons
- Economy and progression records such as balances, XP, jobs, inventory, achievements, factions, and related activity
- Dashboard authentication information necessary to verify access through Discord OAuth or admin-token access
- Billing-related guild license information such as guild ID, plan, subscription status, Stripe customer ID, subscription ID, price ID, and renewal timestamps

Atlas does not intentionally collect more information than is reasonably necessary to operate its features.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use information processed by Atlas to:

- operate bot commands and moderation systems
- detect spam, suspicious links, raids, and security threats
- maintain warnings, cases, logs, notes, appeals, and ticket history
- provide economy, progression, and community features
- authenticate dashboard users and restrict access to authorized server staff
- manage premium licensing and billing status
- improve reliability, safety, and support for Atlas

## 4. How Information Is Stored

Atlas may store information locally in bot data files, server infrastructure, dashboards, logs, and connected billing systems necessary to operate the product.

Depending on deployment, stored data may include files such as:

- warnings
- cases
- moderation records
- ticket history
- economy records
- settings
- license records
- audit records

## 5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal data processed by Atlas.

Information may be shared only in the following limited situations:

- with Discord as part of normal bot operation on the Discord platform
- with service providers or infrastructure used to host and operate Atlas
- with Stripe or other billing providers for subscription and payment processing
- when required by law, regulation, legal process, or valid governmental request
- when necessary to protect the security, rights, or operation of Atlas, its users, or affected servers

## 6. Data Retention

Atlas retains information only as long as reasonably necessary to provide its features, maintain server history, support moderation workflows, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce policies, or protect the service.

Retention may vary depending on:

- the type of feature involved
- server configuration
- whether the data is required for billing, moderation, or audit history
- applicable legal requirements

## 7. User and Server Controls

Server administrators may configure, reset, or remove some categories of Atlas data through commands or the dashboard.

Users or server administrators may request deletion of applicable Atlas data by contacting the Atlas support team. We may need to verify identity or server authority before completing such a request.

## 8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect data processed by Atlas from unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. No system can be guaranteed 100% secure, and use of Atlas is at your own risk.

## 9. Children

Atlas is intended for use on Discord by users who meet Discord's age requirements. Atlas is not directed to children under the age permitted by Discord or applicable law.

## 10. Third-Party Services

Atlas may rely on third-party services including Discord and Stripe. Their handling of information is governed by their own terms and privacy policies.

- Discord Privacy Policy: https://discord.com/privacy
- Discord Terms of Service: https://discord.com/terms
- Stripe Privacy Center: https://stripe.com/privacy

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of Atlas after changes become effective means you accept the updated policy.

## 12. Contact

For privacy questions, support requests, or deletion requests, contact the Atlas support server or the Atlas team through the official support channels listed in the Discord Developer Portal or Atlas product page.
